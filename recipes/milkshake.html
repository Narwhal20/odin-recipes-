Milkshake Recipe

This is the best Milkshake recipe on the internet. It makes 2-4 Milkshakes It pairs very well with the other recipies linked on the home page. You can always substitiute for what you have in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Whole Milk
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Malt Powder
  • Vanilla Beans
  • Whipped Cream
  • Sprinkles

Steps

  1. In a Blender put 2-4 scoops of ice cream, 1.5 cups of milk, 2 tbs of Malt, and 1 tsp of Vanilla Beans
  2. Blend for 1-2 minetes
  3. Pour into a fancy glass
  4. Top with whipped cream and Sprinkles
  5. Enjoy!
