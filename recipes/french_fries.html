French Fries Recipe

This is the best fries recipe on the internet. It makes 2-4 orders of fries It pairs very well with the other recipies linked on the home page. You can always substitiute for what you have in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Seasonings
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Wash and cut your potatos.
  2. Soak potatos in cold ice water for 20 minutes.
  3. Dry potatos then fry them in a pot of oil for 15-20 minutes
  4. Take them out and season with salt, or other seasonings of choice.
  5. Add your Ketchup and Enjoy!
