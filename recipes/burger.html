Burger Recipe

This is the best burger recipe on the internet. It makes 2-4 burgers It pairs very well with the other recipies linked on the home page. You can always substitiute for what you have in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 85% lean ground beef
  • Oil or Butter
  • Slices of American Cheese
  • Burger Buns
  • Lettice
  • Pickles
  • Condiments of Choice

Steps

  1. Divide your beef into patties about half a pound each.
  2. Heat up your pan with oil or butter.
  3. Cook for 5-10 minetes each side.
  4. Once all burgers are done cooking take your buns and crisp them up in the oil.
  5. Assemble your burger, buns, condiments then lettice, pickles, burger top with cheese and finish with the top bun
